My Story

Hello everyone! My name is Marisol, and I’m from Paradise, Texas. I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computing Applications with a minor in Anthropology at Texas Tech University, and I plan to graduate in Spring 2027.

I stepped away from school for several years during COVID and found a career in the insurance industry, but I recently returned to finish my degree. Outside of academics, I enjoy reading and traveling, especially anything that involves exploring new places or theme parks.

One of my favorite memories is a family trip to Universal Studios, where we stood in front of Super Nintendo World to celebrate my brother’s high school graduation. It was my first time visiting, and sharing that experience with family made it even more special. A fun fact about me is that I really enjoy planning trips and creating detailed itineraries almost as much as the travel itself.
